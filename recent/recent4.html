Global Networking: An Endeavor to Mathematical Exposure

Bibas Basnet
on January 29, 2022

With the thought of flourishing the future generation, our Public Relations team, using cold email, reaches out to IMO participants, IYMC finalists, and university professors for quality guidance in complex math topics related to International Math competitions. The connection of Math enthusiasts with them will surely be fruitful. Not only that, our Public Relations Team and Graphic Designers will collaborate to conduct guidance and information webinars covering specific Math topics. Furthermore, such resources can't be limited to a particular range of students. So, our Public Relations team, with our rural outreach program, shall tutor underprivileged students and provide various mathematical resources created by our Graphic Designers and the Content team to them.


More detailed information on this recent work will be out very soon.
