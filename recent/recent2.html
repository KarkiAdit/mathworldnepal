Rural tutoring at Bloom Nepal School: An outreach to serve underprivileged students

Jessica Shrestha
on November 20, 2021

Rural outreach program—one of Math world Nepal’s core projects aims to provide free tutoring and mentorship to students experiencing educational disadvantage in rural and remote communities in Nepal. To jump start our rural outreach project, members from Math World Nepal conducted various sessions on Competitive mathematics at BloomEd Nepal school for underprivileged children.

More detailed information on this recent work will be out very soon.
