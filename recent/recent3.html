E-learning Module: Digitizing the World of Math

Aditya Karki
on November 25, 2021

Our technical team, compromising skilled front-end developers, back-end developers, full-stack developers, and database admin, is creating a digital platform for the discussion and learning of Math resources related to International Math competitions. Our objective is to build a website that holds all the resources created by the Content Team while also adding fun and engaging game-based problems. These games, targeted for international math competition preparations, are uploaded at the of each month to tickle your love. Again, Public Relations Team will reach out through our YouTube channel, Facebook page, and Instagram so that many know that this awesome content is within their grasp. Future awaits a great free resource for mathematical prosperity.

More detailed information on this recent work will be out very soon.
