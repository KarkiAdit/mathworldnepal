MWN's commitment for the International Youth Math Challenge

Have you ever come across a thought that you had that one opportunity to show your mathematical ability? If yes, International Youth Math Challenge (IYMC) has given you the perfect platform to fulfill these inner aspirations. With over ten thousand math enthusiasts from almost hundred countries, IYMC engages you in a fierce, yet friendly, competition worldwide...
MWN wants to help students prepare for this event. To make this possible, we are creating contents of topics that are closely related to the syllabus that IYMC tries to cover. Not only will these contents help learners understand the topics better, but also make them confident in solving various math questions...
